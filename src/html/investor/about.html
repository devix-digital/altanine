business model

Altanine is a global biopharmaceutical company changing patients’ lives in over 120 countries by finding new and affordable ways to treat diabetes and other illnesses.

Altanine business model

  • Altanine has engaged Polomar Specialty Pharmacy as its initial compounding pharmacy.
  • Polomar will pay Altanine a fixed royalty for every prescription of Altanine products that it fulfills.
  • As demand warrants, additional compounding pharmacies can be introduced to help fulfill demand for Altanine products.
  • Telehealth platforms that “white label” Altanine products may have their own compounding pharmacies.

Production

Through a Compounding Pharmacy

Distribution

Doctor’s prescription filled and delivered to the patient

Reimbursement

The Compounding Pharmacy bills the insurance company and/or the patient

Additional Pharmacies

Will be brought on to help manage the patient demand, etc.
