Effectively navigating the FDA's regulatory landscape involves strict compliance

Metformin, Liraglutide, Tirzepatide, Sildenafil and Sumatriptan are proven safe and effective and have been approved by FDA.

Altanine can seek a biowaiver from FDA and file an abbreviated new drug application (ANDA) for Metformin gummies.

Altanine can also seek FDA approval for oral Tirzepatide and Liraglutide as well as inhalable Sildenafil and Sumatriptan.
