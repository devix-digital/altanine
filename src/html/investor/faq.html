What is the mission of Altanine?

Altanine is dedicated to advancing medicine by bringing meaningful improvements to patients' lives through innovative science and delivery technologies for established drug products.

Can you explain Altanine's technology and its benefits?

Altanine leverages advanced proprietary technology, including microencapsulation and enteric coating, to ensure targeted delivery, enhanced drug stability, reduced gastrointestinal side effects, and improved bioavailability of medications.

How does Altanine plan to commercialize its products?

Altanine aims to commercialize its products through direct sales to compounding pharmacies, licensing opportunities, partnerships with telehealth platforms and specialty clinics, and seeking FDA generic approvals for wider distribution.

What innovations is Altanine bringing to the weight loss medication market?

Altanine's technology offers a significant improvement over existing treatments by eliminating the need for injections, using proprietary oral capsule formulations that enhance bioavailability and reduce side effects, making weight loss medication more effective and accessible.

How do Altanine's delivery technologies improve on existing treatments?

Altanine's proprietary enteric coating and microencapsulation technologies enable targeted medication delivery, reducing side effects and enhancing bioavailability, thereby offering superior therapeutic outcomes and patient experience.
