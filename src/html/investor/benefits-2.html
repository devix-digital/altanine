Entry Plan

Go-to-market strategy

01

Medical Outreach for Innovative Metformin Solution

Presentations to doctors, diabetes specialists and diabetes-related medical conferences to introduce a low cost, highly effective solution for metformin administration and patient compliance.

02

Offer Altanine products on a “white label” basis to telehealth platforms as well as specialty clinics.

This includes Metformin and Liraglutide products.

03

The rollout will expand internationally.

Altanine will seek out international licensing partners for companies that wish to manufacture and offer Altanine products in international markets.
