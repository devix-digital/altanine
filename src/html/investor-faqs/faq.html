What does Altanine do?

Altanine is a specialty pharmaceutical company focused on enhancing drug delivery systems. Utilizing proprietary enteric coating and microencapsulation technologies, the company aims to improve the bioavailability of medications, reduce side effects, and enhance patient comfort.

What differentiates Altanine's technology?

The company's proprietary enteric coating technology allows for targeted drug release in the intestine, bypassing stomach degradation. This approach enhances drug stability, improves absorption, and potentially reduces gastrointestinal side effects.
