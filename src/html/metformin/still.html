But still

Metformin remains the first choice of medication for many insulin resistance syndromes because of its effect on weight.

In the Diabetes Prevention Program research study (4), metformin reduced weight by a mean 2.9 kg, and this effect persisted up to 8 years.

Metformin-associated weight loss begins with initiation of the medication and is beneficial in obese individuals with or without diabetes.

However, weight loss can become an unintended adverse side effect in lean individuals with type 2 diabetes.
